Why Pure

A tutor that remembers everything you've learned.

Prerequisite-aware AI

The copilot knows exactly which concepts you've already mastered. No more "as you know from chapter 3" when you haven't read chapter 3.

Three explanation depths

Same concept at three levels — beginner intuition, working-pro detail, expert nuance. "I don't get it" goes deeper. "Simpler" pulls back.

Unlocking, not gating

Mastering a topic unlocks its dependents. You always see exactly what comes next, and why — no mystery prerequisites.

Spaced & contextual

Pure quizzes you on what you've learned — but always in the context of what you're learning now. Forgetting is part of the design.

Jump anywhere with ⌘K

Search any concept across any course. Pure's copilot picks up the conversation wherever you left it — even weeks later.

Progress you can see

Your graph fills in over weeks. A real picture of what you know, exportable as a transcript. Useful for interviews, scholarships, you.
